### The World of CookieRun Classic

**CookieRun Classic** is the origin of the beloved CookieRun series, which has charmed gamers globally. The game is a casual running adventure where you help adorable cookies eat jellies, avoid obstacles, and escape the clutches of the Witch's Oven. The gameplay is straightforward, with super simple controls that make it accessible to players of all ages.

#### Casual Running Action

The core gameplay in **CookieRun Classic** revolves around casual running action. With just two buttons—jump and slide—you'll navigate through levels filled with delectable challenges. The game's controls are intuitive, allowing players to focus on the excitement of the adventure rather than getting lost in complex mechanics.

#### Exclusive K-Culture Cookies

One of the standout features of **CookieRun Classic** is its exclusive K-Culture cookies. These delightful characters are inspired by Korean culture and cuisine, adding a unique touch to the game. From the cute Lotus Root Phantom Cookie to the Lotus Sitter, each cookie brings its own charm and playstyle.

### Collecting and Upgrading

In **CookieRun Classic**, you'll collect your favorite cookies and pets. As you progress, you can upgrade your treasures to create more powerful combinations. This collectible aspect adds depth to the game, encouraging players to explore and experiment with different strategies.

#### Real-Time Competitions

For those who strive to be the very best, **CookieRun Classic** offers real-time competitions. Enter the League and compete with players worldwide for the highest score. This feature adds a competitive edge to the game, keeping players engaged and striving for greatness.

### Welcoming New Runners

**CookieRun Classic** is welcoming to all runners, regardless of their experience level. New players can get a head start with special events and buffs designed just for them. This inclusive approach ensures that everyone can enjoy the adventure, from beginners to seasoned gamers.
