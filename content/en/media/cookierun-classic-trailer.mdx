## The Origin of the CookieRun Series

CookieRun Classic is the origin story of the beloved CookieRun series, which has won the affection of players across the globe. This game follows the escapades of adorable cookies as they navigate through a whimsical world filled with obstacles and dangers. The goal? To escape the Witch's Oven and embark on a series of exciting adventures.

### Casual Running Action with Super Simple Controls

One of the standout features of CookieRun Classic is its casual running action. The controls are incredibly simple, requiring only two buttons: jump and slide. This makes the game accessible to players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the world of mobile gaming, you'll find yourself quickly picking up the rhythm and enjoying the fast-paced action.

## Exclusive K-Culture Cookies

CookieRun Classic takes its inspiration from Korean culture and cuisine, offering players the chance to meet cookies inspired by these elements. From the iconic Dalkgalbi Cookie to the mouth-watering Tteokbokki Cookie, each character is unique and brings its own charm to the game. This exclusive K-Culture twist adds an extra layer of depth to the game, making it stand out from its competitors.

### Ever New Cookies, Pets, and Treasures!

CookieRun Classic is constantly evolving, with new cookies, pets, and treasures being introduced regularly.
