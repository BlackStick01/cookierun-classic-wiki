### How to Upgrade Classic Treasures in Cookie Run Kingdom

Upgrading classic treasures in Cookie Run Kingdom is a straightforward process. Follow these steps:

1. **Collect Resources**: Gather the necessary resources for the upgrade, such as cookies, candy, and gold.
2. **Access the Upgrade Menu**: Open the treasure upgrade menu in your character's inventory.
3. **Select the Treasure**: Choose the classic treasure you wish to upgrade.
4. **Allocate Resources**: Assign the collected resources to the upgrade.
5. **Confirm the Upgrade**: Once you've allocated the resources, confirm the upgrade, and watch your character's stats improve.
