### How Treasures Enhance Your Run

Treasures in CookieRun Classic can be equipped in addition to Cookies and Pets, providing unique benefits that can turn the tide of battle. They can be categorized by Release, Level, Rarity, and more, and each treasure has its own set of Enhancements that can increase its score and grant bonus effects.

## The Best Treasures in CookieRun Classic

Special treasures offer unique mechanics that can change the dynamics of your run. Some notable examples include:

Epic and Legendary treasures are the pinnacle of the treasure collection, offering the most powerful abilities. While they are rare, they are well worth the effort to obtain:

POWER+ treasures are the ultimate collectibles in CookieRun Classic, offering the most powerful abilities and enhancements. Some of the best POWER+ treasures include:

## Enhancements: The Secret to Unlocking Treasure Power

Enhancements are a crucial aspect of treasure customization in CookieRun Classic. By enhancing your treasures, you can increase their score and unlock bonus effects. Here are some of the best enhancements to consider:

## FAQs

**Q: How many treasures can I equip in a run?**
A: You can equip up to three different treasures in any run of CookieRun Classic.

**Q: Can I upgrade my treasures?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade your treasures by collecting treasure fragments and using them to enhance your treasures.

**Q: Are there any treasure-specific guides available?**
A: Yes, there are many player experience guides and community reports available online that provide detailed information on the best treasures to use in different situations.

**Q: Can treasures be used in any Cookie Run game?**
