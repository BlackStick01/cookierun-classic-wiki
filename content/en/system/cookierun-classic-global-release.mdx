### Easy as Pie: Controls and Gameplay

One of the standout features of CookieRun Classic is its super simple controls. All you need are two buttons to jump and slide, making it easy for players of all ages and skill levels to dive into the action. This casual running game offers a perfect blend of relaxation and challenge, making it a perfect pastime for a quick escape from reality.
