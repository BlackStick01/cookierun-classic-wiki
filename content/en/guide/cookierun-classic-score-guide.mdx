## Understanding the Basics

CookieRun Classic is a thrilling endless runner where you control a cookie as it dodges obstacles, collects coins, and jellies, and scores points. To start your journey, familiarize yourself with the gameplay and controls. The core mechanics are simple: jump to avoid obstacles and slide under them. Mastering these controls is the first step to a higher score.

### Gameplay and Controls

The gameplay in CookieRun Classic is straightforward. You control your character with just two main actions: jump and slide. Practice these movements to navigate through the various levels more effectively.

- **Jump:** Press the jump button to leap over obstacles.
- **Slide:** Press the slide button to slide under low obstacles.

By mastering these controls, you'll be able to navigate through the levels with ease, collecting as many coins and jellies as possible.

| Rarity | Description |

## Forge Your Cookie Arsenal: Treasures and Boosts

Each cookie you choose for runs in CookieRun Classic can be equipped with three treasures and three boosts to help you run longer and score higher points.

### Treasures

Treasures are special items that you can equip for your cookie during runs. While their effects are not very huge, they are very helpful for boosting performance and helping you survive longer.

| Treasure | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| Speed Boost | Increases your cookie's speed. |
| Coin Multiplier | Doubles the coins you collect. |
