## Master the CookieRun Classic: Essential Tips for Beginners

Are you ready to embark on a sweet adventure in the world of CookieRun Classic? This endless running game has taken the gaming community by storm, and with its charming characters and thrilling challenges, it's no wonder why. Whether you're a seasoned runner or a fresh-faced gamer, our beginner's guide to CookieRun Classic will equip you with the essential tips and tricks to get a strong start and run like a pro.

## Gameplay and Controls: Navigating the CookieWorld

The first step to mastering CookieRun Classic is understanding the gameplay and controls. This game is all about timing and skill, so getting a handle on the basics is crucial. You'll be using two main controls: Jump and Slide. Jump to leap over obstacles or reach higher platforms, and slide to dodge low-hanging hazards. Mastering these movements will help you survive longer and score higher.

### Episodes: The Stages of Your Adventure

Your endless running journey in CookieRun Classic is divided into episodes, each with its own unique theme, environment, and set of obstacles. These episodes are the main stages of the game, and each run costs one Heart. As you progress, the challenges will become more difficult, requiring better timing and control to achieve higher scores.

### Collectibles: The Rewards of Your Runs

As you run through the episodes, your main goal is to collect rewards scattered throughout the stages. These include:

- **Coins**: The primary currency used for upgrades and progression.
- **Jellies**: The main source of points, crucial for achieving high scores.
- **Mystery Boxes**: Special rewards that can contain valuable resources such as Coins, Crystals, and Cookie unlock materials.

## Understanding Your Running Cookies: Grades and Leveling Up

Before diving into the episodes, it's important to understand your running cookies. These are your playable runners, each with unique skills that can help you collect more jellies, earn extra coins, destroy obstacles, or survive longer.

### Cookies Rarities: Unlocking the Power of Your Characters

Cookies come in different rarities, and upgrading them makes them even stronger. Here's a breakdown of the rarity system:

| Rarity | Description |
| --- | --- |
| E-Grade | Special limited-edition or commemorative cookies |
| C-Grade | Very basic starter cookies |
| B-Grade | Better cookies with simple effects |
| A-Grade | Stronger cookies that help with speed boosts, revives, and obstacle breaking |
| S-Grade | High-tier cookies with powerful and unique effects |
| SS-Grade | Very powerful cookies obtained through special bundles and upgraded with soulstones |
| L-Grade | Rare special cookies obtained through the Jewel Puzzle gacha |

### Leveling Up Your Cookies: A Step-by-Step Guide

Having powerful cookies is just the beginning. You can also upgrade them to higher levels to improve performance in runs. Cookies can be leveled up to Level 8 using Coins collected during gameplay. Higher levels increase energy and make their skills stronger.

**Tip:** Don't spend coins on every cookie. Focus on upgrading 2-3 strong cookies to get better results faster.

## Choose Your Pet Partner: Mighty Companions for Big Runs

Pets can be upgraded and become even more powerful over time. You can level them up using coins and obtain stronger ones through hatching Pet Eggs. To get the best advantage in runs, make sure to match pets with your cookie's playstyle, upgrade them wisely, and always aim for the best combinations.

## Forge Your Cookie Arsenal: Treasures and Boosts

Each cookie you choose for runs in CookieRun Classic can be equipped with three treasures and three boosts to help you run longer and score higher points. Here's how they work:

### Treasures

- **Special items that you can equip for your cookie during runs**.
- **Their effects are not very huge, but they are very helpful for boosting performance and helping you survive longer**.
- Choose and equip treasures based on your cookie's strengths and the type of run you are doing, such as coin farming or high-scoring runs.

### Boosts

- **One-time power-ups you choose before a run**.
- **Boosters come with effects like extra energy, coin multipliers, relays, score boosters, revival chances, and more**.
- **Save them for tougher situations, high-score attempts, or coin-farming runs to get the best advantage**.

## Upgrade Energy, Jellies, and Bonus Time: Boost Your Runs

As you progress in CookieRun Classic, there are three special features: Energy, Jellies, and Bonus Time that can greatly improve every single run you make. You can earn upgrade points for these features through runs by completing goals and leveling up. Here's how they work:

| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Energy** | Increases your maximum energy for your cookies and improves how efficiently you use it. This helps with longer runs, allows you to collect more items during runs, and lets you play more without waiting for Hearts to recharge. |
| **Jellies** | Great for Top Scores. Upgrading Jellies helps improve jelly collection and increases the points you earn. It becomes more important when you're aiming to reach higher positions on the leaderboards. |
| **Bonus Time** | Important for Top Scores. Bonus Time is a special flying phase where you do not lose energy for a short period. You can also collect massive amounts of jellies and coins during this phase. Upgrading it helps Bonus Time trigger more often, last longer, and allows you to collect more rewards. |

## Build Your Friends List: More Friends, More Lives

Friends are one of the most useful features in CookieRun Classic. You can add lots of friends, and you can also compete against them to see who is the best and who scores the highest. This makes your gameplay more fun and exciting.

However, the most important advantage of adding active players to your friends list is sharing Lives. Friends can send Lives (Hearts), and you can also send Lives every day. These Lives can stack without a limit when sent by friends. This special feature is only available when you have friends; otherwise, you can only hold up to 5 Lives and must wait until they refill again. This means that having more friends allows you to keep running without waiting for your Hearts to recharge.

## FAQs

**Q: What is CookieRun Classic?**
A: CookieRun Classic is an endless running game where you control Cookies as they run through different stages. Your goal is to avoid obstacles, collect coins and jellies, and score as high as possible in each run.

**Q: What are the best Cookies for CookieRun Classic beginners?**
A: As a beginner, start with C-Grade and B-Grade Cookies. They are easy to get and useful early on. Focus on 2-3 main Cookies, upgrade them to Level 8, and pair them with Pets for better coins, jellies, and survival.

**Q: How do I get higher scores in the CookieRun Classic?**
A: To score higher in CookieRun Classic, try to stay alive longer by mastering jumps and slides, collect as many jellies and coins as possible, and upgrade your Cookies, Pets, Energy, and Bonus Time regularly.

By following these tips and strategies, you'll be well on your way to becoming a CookieRun Classic champion. Happy running!
