## Mastering CookieRun Classic: The Ultimate Beginner's Guide

Embarking on a new adventure in the whimsical world of CookieRun Classic can be both exciting and daunting. With its array of characters, pets, and treasures, the game offers a rich experience for players of all levels. This guide is tailored to help beginners navigate the game's mechanics, maximize their resources, and enjoy a strategic and rewarding gameplay experience. So, lace up your virtual boots and let's dive into the CookieRun Classic beginner's guide!

## Understanding the Basics: Cookies, Pets, and Treasures

Before you can embark on your cookie-powered journey, it's essential to understand the core elements that make up the game. Cookies are the characters you control, each with unique abilities that can significantly impact gameplay. These characters are graded from C to S, with S-grade cookies generally boasting superior abilities. As you level up, you'll unlock new cookies to add to your team.

### Cookies

- **What are cookies?** Cookies are the playable characters in the game, each with distinct abilities affecting gameplay. They are ranked from C to S, with S-grade cookies having better abilities.
- **How do you get cookies?** You can only obtain cookies by leveling up. As you progress, you'll unlock a variety of cookies to customize your team.

### Pets

- **What are pets?** Pets are small creatures that follow your cookie during the run. They have their own abilities and are also ranked from C to S.
- **How do you get pets?** Pets can be hatched from eggs or found in the Pets Lucky Draw. Using a specific pet with a cookie can unlock special bonuses.

### Treasures

- **What are treasures?** Treasures are objects that can aid gameplay, found in treasure chests. They have ranks and can be upgraded and evolved to enhance their effects.
- **How do you get treasures?** Treasures are found randomly in treasure chests. They can be upgraded and evolved to improve their effects.

## Starting the Game: Life, Currency, and Friends

Once you've grasped the basics, it's time to start your adventure. Here's what you need to know about life, currency, and making friends in CookieRun Classic.

### Life

- **What is the life meter?** The life meter is a bar on the top right corner with hearts, representing your energy. Each game you play depletes one heart, and you must wait for it to replenish.
- **How can you increase your life?** You can increase your life by inviting friends, sending/receiving lives, and using notifications.

### Currency

- **Coins** are the standard currency, easily obtainable by playing and from Gifts Lucky Draw.
- **Crystals** are the premium currency, harder to obtain but crucial for purchasing powerful items like cookies, pets, and treasures.

### Friends

- **Why are friends important?** Friends can help you increase your life, provide crystals, and share strategies.
- **How do you make friends?** You can invite friends through the game's social features and participate in community events.

## Maximizing Your Resources: Spending Coins and Crystals Wisely

With a basic understanding of the game's mechanics, it's time to focus on maximizing your resources. This section outlines how to spend your coins and crystals wisely.

### Spending Coins

- **Energy upgrades** are essential for extending your playtime.
- **Buttercream Choco Cookie** is a great investment for beginners, offering extra coins per run.
- **Treasure upgrades** should focus on S-rank treasures, though they come with a high risk of failure.

### Spending Crystals

- **Angel Cookie** is a must-have due to its treasure, Angel Cookie's Holy Feather, providing Magnetic Aura.
- **Mint Choco Cookie** is another excellent choice for coin farming.
- **Extra Rare Eggs** are the only pet eggs you should consider buying.

## Strategies for Success: Coin Farming, Auto Farming, and XP Farming

Once you have a grasp of the game's basics and have built up your resources, it's time to implement strategies for success. Here are three key strategies to consider:

### Coin Farming

- **Use Mint Choco Cookie as the main cookie** and Buttercream Choco Cookie as a relay.
- **Equip the Coin Scale pet** to convert points into coins.
- **Combine with the right treasures** to maximize coin production.

### Auto Farming

- **Soda Cookie** is ideal for auto farming, as his soda waves help the cookie cross holes safely while knocking away obstacles.

### XP Farming

- **Focus on earning XP** instead of coins for higher-level players.
- **Use treasures that provide extra XP** to extend your runs and gain more experience.

## Tips and Tricks for High Scores

Achieving high scores in CookieRun Classic requires a combination of skill, strategy, and a bit of luck. Here are some tips and tricks to help you climb the leaderboard:

- **Avoid obstacles** to prevent energy loss.
- **Equip treasures that slow energy drain** and provide Magnetic Aura.
- **Use the Angel Cookie's Rainbow Feather treasure** for additional points from jellies.
