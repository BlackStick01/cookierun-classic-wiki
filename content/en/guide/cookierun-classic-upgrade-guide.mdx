## Maximize Your Runs: The Ultimate CookieRun Classic Upgrade Guide

Are you ready to take your CookieRun Classic experience to the next level? Whether you're a seasoned runner or just starting out, mastering the art of upgrading your Cookies and pets is key to scoring big and enjoying every run. In this comprehensive guide, we'll delve into the ins and outs of CookieRun Classic upgrades, providing you with actionable tips and strategies to help you dominate the endless running game.

### Understanding the Basics

Before diving into the upgrade process, it's essential to understand the basics of CookieRun Classic. The game features a simple yet engaging gameplay loop: run, jump, slide, and collect. As you progress through various episodes, you'll encounter different challenges and obstacles that test your timing and skill. To excel, you'll need to collect coins, jellies, and unlock new features.

### Gameplay and Controls

CookieRun Classic is easy to pick up but hard to master. The primary controls are Jump and Slide. Mastering these actions will help you navigate the game's many obstacles and collect valuable rewards. Practice performing double jumps to reach higher platforms and learn when to slide to avoid low obstacles. With these simple controls, you'll be well on your way to surviving longer and scoring more points.

### Understanding Your Running Cookies: Grades and Leveling Up

Your Cookies are your most valuable assets in CookieRun Classic. Each Cookie has a unique skill that activates during gameplay, helping you collect more jellies, earn extra coins, destroy obstacles, or survive longer. Cookies come in different rarities, and upgrading them makes them even stronger.

#### Cookies Rarities

- **E-Grade:** Special limited-edition or commemorative Cookies released during events and celebrations.
- **C-Grade:** Very basic starter Cookies that new players receive early in the game.
- **B-Grade:** Better Cookies with simple effects like coin boosts or bonus time.
- **A-Grade:** Stronger Cookies that help with speed boosts, revives, and obstacle breaking.
- **S-Grade:** High-tier Cookies with powerful and unique effects that improve scoring, survival, and resource collection.
- **SS-Grade:** Very powerful Cookies in your roster. They are hard to obtain through challenges, events, or special bundles and are upgraded using soulstones.
- **L-Grade:** Rare special Cookies obtained through the Jewel Puzzle gacha using Episode Jewels from Mystery Boxes.

#### Leveling Up Your Cookies

Upgrading your Cookies is crucial for improving your performance in runs. Cookies can be leveled up to Level 8 using Coins collected during gameplay. Higher levels increase energy and make their skills stronger. Remember, don't spend coins on every cookie. Focus on upgrading 2–3 strong Cookies to get better results faster.

### Choose Your Pet Partner: Mighty Companions for Big Runs

Pets can be upgraded and become even more powerful over time. You can level them up using coins and obtain stronger ones through hatching Pet Eggs. To get the best advantage in runs, make sure to match Pets with your Cookie’s playstyle, upgrade them wisely, and always aim for the best combinations.

### Forge Your Cookie Arsenal: Treasures and Boosts

Each Cookie you choose for runs in CookieRun Classic can be equipped with three Treasures and three Boosts to help you run longer and score higher points. Treasures are special items that you can equip for your Cookie during runs, while Boosts are one-time power-ups you choose before a run. Both can significantly impact your performance, so choose wisely.

### Upgrade Energy, Jellies, and Bonus Time: Boost Your Runs

As you progress in CookieRun Classic, there are three special features: Energy, Jellies, and Bonus Time that can greatly improve every single run you make. You can earn upgrade points for these features through runs by completing goals and leveling up. Spend these points wisely to maximize your benefits.

#### Best Upgrade Order for Beginners

- **Energy:** Highest Priority. Upgrading Energy increases your maximum energy for your Cookies and improves how efficiently you use it.
- **Jellies:** Great for Top Scores. Upgrading Jellies helps improve jelly collection and increases the points you earn.
- **Bonus Time:** Important for Top Scores. Bonus Time is a special flying phase where you do not lose energy for a short period.

### Build Your Friends List: More Friends, More Lives

Friends are one of the most useful features in CookieRun Classic. You can add lots of friends, and you can also compete against them to see who is the best and who scores the highest. The most important advantage of adding active players to your friends list is sharing Lives. Friends can send Lives (Hearts), and you can also send Lives every day. This means that having more friends allows you to keep running without waiting for your Hearts to recharge.

### Conclusion

By following the tips and strategies outlined in this guide, you'll be well on your way to mastering CookieRun Classic upgrades and achieving top scores. Remember to focus on upgrading your Cookies and pets, choose the right Treasures and Boosts, and build a strong friends list to maximize your gaming experience. Happy running!

### FAQs

**Q: What is the best way to upgrade my Cookies in CookieRun Classic?**
A: Prioritize upgrading your strongest Cookies, focusing on those that complement your playstyle and offer the most significant benefits.

**Q: How can I get more Jellies in CookieRun Classic?**
A: Collect Jellies by jumping and sliding through the game's stages. Upgrading your Jellies will also help you collect more of them during runs.

**Q: What are the best Boosts to use in CookieRun Classic?**
A: Choose Boosts based on your current run's needs. For example, use energy boosters in tough situations or score boosters when aiming for high scores.

**Q: How can I build a strong friends list in CookieRun Classic?**
A: Add active players to your friends list, as they can provide valuable support and resources, such as sending Lives (Hearts) to help you keep running.
