## Jump and Slide: The Two-Button Control System

CookieRun Classic is designed with simplicity in mind, using only two controls: jump and slide. However, don't let the ease of use fool you; the skill ceiling is high, and mastering these controls is crucial for success.

### Practice Double Jumps

One of the first things you should learn is the double jump. Many collectibles and safe landing zones are just out of reach with a single jump. By timing your second tap perfectly, you can extend your reach and collect more Jellies. This skill can save you countless runs in the long run.

- **Tap Jump once** for standard platforms.
- **Tap Jump twice quickly** for higher collectibles and safer landings.

### Slide Early and Often

When encountering low obstacles, it's better to slide early rather than react late. The game's hitboxes punish hesitation, so being proactive is key. Remember to release the slide early if the obstacle ends before you expect to avoid unnecessary damage.

| Control | Action | Purpose |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Jump | Single tap | Standard platform |
| Jump | Double tap | Higher collectibles and safer landings |
| Slide | Hold | Navigate through extended low sections |
| Slide | Release early | Avoid unnecessary damage |

## Choosing the Right Cookie: A Beginner's Guide

As a new player, you'll be introduced to various Cookie rarities. While it's tempting to chase rare drops, it's more beneficial to focus on C-Grade and B-Grade Cookies early on.

### Why C-Grade and B-Grade Cookies?

These Cookies drop frequently, level up cheaply, and carry useful capacit?s that don't require heavy investment. A levelled common Cookie outperforms a level 1 rare Cookie every time. Save your premium currency and rare resources for later when you can experiment with specialized builds.

| Cookie Rarity | Frequency | Leveling Cost | Abilities | Best for |
|---------------|-----------|---------------|-----------|----------|
| C-Grade | High | Low | Useful | New Players |
| B-Grade | Moderate | Moderate | Useful | New Players |
| Rare | Low | High | Unique | Advanced Players |

## Prioritizing Pets and Treasures

In CookieRun Classic, Pets and Treasures can significantly impact your performance. Here's how to prioritize them effectively.

### Pets: Your Passive Allies

Pets follow your Cookie and provide passive benefits. Some collect missed Jellies, while others extend Energy or boost score multipliers. Choose a Pet that complements your current Cookie or playstyle.

### Treasures: Your Active Boosts

Treasures provide active or passive bonuses and can create powerful combinations with specific Cookies. However, upgrading Treasures can be expensive. Focus on one Treasure that complements your playstyle and max it out before diversifying.

| Treasure Type | Effect | Priority |
|---------------|--------|----------|
| Energy Extender | Extend Energy duration | High |
| Jelly Collector | Increase Jelly collection radius | High |
| Coin Boost | Boost coin gains | High |
| Damage Protection | Reduce damage | Low |

## Managing Your Resources: Coins and Upgrades

Coins are earned through runs, daily rewards, and Mystery Boxes. It's important to manage your resources wisely.

### Prioritize Energy and Jelly Upgrades

Upgrade Energy first to extend your runs and increase your rewards. Next, focus on Jelly upgrades to help you hit reward thresholds in events and Leagues. Bonus Time upgrades become more important once your base runs last long enough to reach Bonus Time consistently.

| Resource | Purpose | Priority |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Energy | Extend runs | High |
| Jelly | Hit reward thresholds | High |
| Bonus Time | Extend runs | High |
| Coins | Purchase upgrades | High |

## Understanding Episodes and Progression

CookieRun Classic features themed Episodes with distinct visual styles and obstacle patterns. Each Episode costs one Heart, making Episode selection a resource decision.

### Episode Strategy

Run easier Episodes when learning new Cookies or testing Treasure combinations. Save harder Episodes for when you need specific rewards or want to push high scores. The difficulty ramps up in later Episodes, so build your skills and upgrades before forcing progress.

| Episode Difficulty | Strategy |
|--------------------|----------|
| Easy | Learn new Cookies, test Treasure combinations |
| Medium | Practice, experiment with builds |
| Hard | Push high scores, gather specific rewards |

## Daily Systems and Free Progress

CookieRun Classic includes events and buffs specifically for new players. These provide accelerated early growth, so be sure to check event timers and claim everything before these windows close.

### Friends and Free Lives

Your Friends list provides extra Lives. More active friends mean more chances to run daily without waiting for natural Heart regeneration. Add friends early and send Lives consistently to benefit from the reciprocal system.

### Mystery Boxes

Mystery Boxes appear during runs and in reward screens. Open these whenever possible to provide random resources that accelerate upgrades without coin cost.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

New players often make mistakes that waste early progress. Here are some common pitfalls to avoid:

- Chasing rare Cookies before levelling commons
- Spreading upgrades across too many Treasures
- Running hard Episodes for pride instead of efficient rewards
- Ignoring the Friends system and missing free Lives
- Spending premium currency on continue attempts instead of permanent upgrades
