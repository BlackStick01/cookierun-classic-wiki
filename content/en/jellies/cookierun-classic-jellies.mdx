## Understanding the Basics of Cookierun Classic Jellies

Cookierun Classic jellies are a diverse group of obstacles that players encounter on their quest to score high. They come in various forms, each with its own set of points and challenges. The most common types of jellies include:

- **Normal Jellies**: These are the standard jelly obstacles that players encounter. They can be upgraded before each game to increase their point value.
- **Bear Jellies**: These jellies come in different stages, each with a higher point value as the game progresses.
- **Coins**: Coins are worth points and can be collected throughout the game.
- **Energy Potions**: These provide players with additional energy and can be worth points depending on their type.
- **Power Jellies**: These jellies provide temporary boosts to the player's abilities.

## The Evolution of Jelly Points in Cookierun Classic

The jelly points system in Cookierun Classic has evolved over the years, with changes occurring in different seasons. As of June 2016, there have been three significant changes to the jelly points system:

- **Season 1**: The initial scoring system.
- **Season 2 and 3 (2nd Story and Edge of the World)**: The points for each jelly were adjusted.
- **Season 4, 5, and 6 (New World, The City of Wizards, and Tower of Frozen Waves)**: Further changes were made to the scoring system.

It's important to note that the points for each jelly will be different during Bonus Time, starting from level 31 and above.
