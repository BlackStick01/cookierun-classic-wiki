## Unlocking the Secrets of CookieRun Classic

Are you ready to dive into the thrilling world of CookieRun Classic? If so, you're in for a treat with the Classic Bonus Time, a special session that can transform your gameplay and boost your score. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about the Classic Bonus Time, including how to earn it, its types, and the best items to help you maximize your score. So, grab your cookie and let's get started!

### Earning Your Classic Bonus Time

To earn a Classic Bonus Time in CookieRun Classic, you have a couple of options. The first is to collect the alphabet letters (B-O-N-U-S-T-I-M-E) that appear in various places during your run. Each letter is worth 230 points, so you'll need to be on the lookout and quick to collect them. The second way is to find the Bonus Time Jelly, which will instantly transport your cookie into the Bonus Time World. This jelly can be found in specific stages, such as Stage 10 in Escape from the Oven and Stage 6 in Dragon's Valley.

### Types of Classic Bonus Time

The Classic Bonus Time comes in three exciting forms, each with its own unique features and challenges:

#### Classic Bonus Time

The classic type of Bonus Time is a fan favorite, featuring yellow and pink bear jellies, silver and gold coins, and occasional appearances of Blast Jelly, Giant Jelly, and Magnet Jelly. These elements help you earn a large amount of points as they spread into few formations. An addition in the "Edge of The World" season even adds flying bear jellies that require precise control to collect.

#### Space Bonus Time

Space Bonus Time introduces coins and bear jellies that move in straight or diagonal lines, creating a challenging and fast-paced experience. This type of Bonus Time requires players to equip magnetic aura to navigate effectively, as the speed can be overwhelming for beginners. Firework jelly may also appear, adding another layer of difficulty.

#### Rainbow Rush Bonus Time

The rarest and most lucrative of the three is the Rainbow Rush Bonus Time, also known as the 'puking-bear' type. It can only be obtained while your health is low, and it's filled with giant gold coins and bear jellies. Players can earn at least 1,000 coins per section, making it a highly sought-after Bonus Time.

### Bonus Time Upgrade

For new players, the initial Classic Bonus Time session is quite short, lasting only 3 seconds. However, you can upgrade the length of your Bonus Time session by investing in Upgrade Powers before playing the game or by equipping items that extend the duration. From Level 31 and above, the upgrade will no longer extend the bonus time length but will instead provide additional bonus points for every jelly obtained during the session.

### Items and Cookies That Boost Bonus Time

To maximize your Classic Bonus Time experience, consider equipping the following items and cookies:

- **Cookies**: Strawberry Cookie gives up to +5 seconds of Bonus Time, while Apple Cookie generates her own Alphabet Jellies enclosed in a bubble.
- **Pets**: Rainbow's End grows up to 4x in Bonus Time, and Mocha Delight makes alphabet jellies up to every 3 seconds.
- **Treasures**: Always Cute Acorn provides extra 0.8~1.7 seconds in Bonus Time, and A Piece of Rainbow's End makes your pet grow 1.5x~2.5x in Bonus Time.

### Player Experience and Community Reports

According to player experience, the Classic Bonus Time is a highly anticipated event, with many players looking forward to the opportunity to earn extra points and coins. Community reports suggest that the most effective strategy is to collect as many alphabet letters as possible before finding the Bonus Time Jelly, as this ensures you don't lose all your progress if you earn incomplete letters before the Jelly appears.
